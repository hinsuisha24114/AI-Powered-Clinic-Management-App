# Doctor Dashboard - Purpose and Explanation

## What is This Page?

This is the **Doctor Dashboard** - the **main home page** that doctors see when they log into the clinic management system. It's **NOT specifically for prescriptions**, but rather an **overview/control center** that shows all important information at a glance.

Think of it like a **doctor's command center** - similar to how a car dashboard shows speed, fuel, and warnings all in one place.

---

## Purpose of This Page

The Dashboard serves as the **main landing page** where doctors can:
1. **See their daily schedule** (appointments)
2. **Monitor patient queue** (who's waiting)
3. **Check notifications** (important updates)
4. **Quickly access common tasks** (add patient, create appointment, write prescription)

---

  - **"Consultation"** button - to start consultation with scheduled patients

---

### 2. **Smart Queue Management Card** (Top Right)
**Purpose:** Shows patients waiting in the queue with estimated wait times

**What it displays:**
- Queue numbers (Q1, Q2, Q3, etc.)
- Patient names
- Estimated wait time (5 min, 8 min, 15 min, etc.)

**Why it's useful:** 
- Doctor knows who's next
- Patients can see how long they'll wait
- Helps manage clinic flow efficiently

---

### 3. **Notifications Card** (Bottom Left)
**Purpose:** Shows important updates and alerts

**What it displays:**
- "Prescriptions shared" - when prescriptions are sent to patients
- "Lab results uploaded" - when test results are available
- "Payment pending" - when bills need attention

**Why it's useful:** Doctor stays informed about important events without checking multiple pages

---

### 4. **Quick Actions Card** (Bottom Right)
**Purpose:** Provides shortcuts to common tasks

**What it displays:**
- **"Add Patient"** button - quickly add new patient to system
- **"Create Appointment"** button - schedule new appointment
- **"New Prescription"** button - write prescription (this is where prescriptions are created!)

**Why it's useful:** Saves time - doctor doesn't need to navigate through menus to do common tasks
